# Calendar & Reminders

## Calendar Management
- Multiple calendars (local, iCal, Google-ready)
- Event CRUD operations
- Calendar sync from iCal URLs
- Upcoming events query

## Reminder System
- Natural language parsing
- Time parsing: "at 3 PM", "in 30 minutes", "tomorrow at 10 AM"
- Recurring reminders: daily, weekly, monthly, yearly
- Voice notifications when reminders trigger

## Voice Commands
- "Remind me to call mom at 3 PM"
- "Remind me to water plants daily at 8 AM"
- "List reminders"

**API**: `/api/v1/calendar/*`
